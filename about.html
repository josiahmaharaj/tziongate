About Tzion

Who We Are

Tzion Gate Life Centre Ministry is a God ordained Organization that was established to improve the quality of life locally, regionally and internationally. The organization was registered as a Non-profit/NGO on 21st July 2016.

Our Vision

“Impacting Lives; spiritually, emotionally, intellectually, physically, socially and financially”.
  1. Spiritually - Teaching Biblical principles to enhance quality of participants’ spiritual life.
  2. Emotionally - The design and implementation of mentoring and counselling programmes to support participants’ emotional health.
  3. Intellectually - Assisting with specific Subject areas of functional weakness: reading, writing, mathematics, grammar, computer literacy, as well as specific focus on Subjects in preparation for CXC and SEA exams.
  4. Socially – Development and enhancement of social skills.
  5. Physically - Provision of options to various Sporting Disciplines for recreation and competition.
  6. Financially – Provision of financial counselling.

Our Mission

“To improve the quality of life through a Five-Fold Ministry of Prayer, Counselling, Teaching – Academics and Theology, Children’s Foundation and a Sporting Foundation”.
  1. Prayer – Mission: Supporting individuals through prayer to meet their daily needs, emotionally, spiritually, physically, financially, intellectually and socially.
  2. Counselling – Mission: Assisting individuals in achieving a balanced life style.
  3. Teaching
    • Academics - Mission: Providing enhanced learning to Secondary School Students preparing for CXC exams in specific subject areas, such as Mathematics, POB.
    • Theology - Mission: To provide a forum to share Biblical principles.
  4. Children’s Foundation – Mission: Providing a Christ-centred environment in which faith can be integrated with learning to equip students to become “spiritually, academically, socially and physically prepared to achieve God’s will and purpose for their lives. The Foundation will be operated in three discrete yet integrated functional areas.
    1. Tzion Gate Homework Academy which will help improve academic/intellectual base of Primary school aged children.
    2. Tzion Gate Sponsorship Programme which will develop support systems to feed, clothe and provide school books and supplies for children.
    3. Tzion Gate Social Centre which will provide avenues for social interaction through games/recreation, music, arts and crafts projects.
  5. Sporting Foundation – Mission: The Sporting Foundation is a two-fold Mission: (1) Finding raw talent and nurturing them through well planned Athletics (Track & Field) Program and a Football Program based on their potential and abilities. (2) Identifying potential talent in the various Sporting Disciplines e.g., Karate, Basketball, Swimming etc., and refer these potential Athletes to our Sporting Partners.

Our Strategy

Our Strategy is our Vision which is to Impact Lives; Spiritually, Emotionally, Intellectually, Physically, Socially and Financially through various Projects & Initiatives. Our target audience is: schools, friends, family, neighbours and communities. Our overall Approach/Methodology will be to:
  • Complete a Project/Initiative at every School Break, i.e., December (Christmas), March/April (Easter), July-August (Summer).
  • for raw talent in Primary and Secondary Schools to recruit Players ‘Tzion Gate Football Academy’ and Athletes for ‘Tzion Gate Athletic Academy’.
  • Select Primary Schools and identify children for the ‘Tzion Gate Homework Academy’.
  • Identify children and families for the ‘Tzion Gate Sponsorship Programme’.
  • Identify children for the ‘Tzion Gate Social Centre’.
  • Develop programmes under the ‘Teaching - (Theology)’ ministry to facilitate sharing Biblical principles.
  • Select children in Secondary Schools for the ‘Teaching - (Academics)’ ministry to benefit from additional lessons.
  • Develop programmes that provides a forum to facilitate Prayer and Counselling.

Our Achievements

Since the launch of Tzion Gate Life Centre Ministry in October 2016, we have completed the following Projects & Initiatives:

  • On 5th June 2018, Tzion Gate Life Centre Ministry in collaboration with Tunapuna Government Primary School has initiated a structured After School programme under the umbrella of Tzion Gate Children’s Foundation – Tzion Gate Homework Academy. The Mission of Tzion Gate Homework Academy - After School Programme is: To improve the intellectual capability of all participants in our classes and to also enhance their spiritual and emotional development through focused teaching and positive interactions with well-trained and professional tutors. In the short to medium term, the program will focus on reviewing and strengthening basic principles of Reading, Writing, English, and Mathematics for Primary School students in classes ranging from 2nd year, Standard 1, Standard 2, Standard 3, and Standard 4.
  • 3rd March 2018 was the official start of the Tzion Gate Football Academy for Youths 8 to 14 years. Our strategy is to scout for raw talent mainly in Primary Schools, and to date, we successfully have 40 + players who have joined the Academy. Currently, there are Players from six (6) Primary Schools; Tunapuna Government, Tunapuna Anglican, Curepe Anglican, San Juan Boys, Arouca Government, and Elohim Learning Centre. Our training grounds is at Constantine Park, Macoya, and our training sessions are held every Saturday from 9:00 am to 12:00 noon. Additionally, Tzion Gate Football Academy is a registered member of EFATT – Eastern Football Association of Trinidad & Tobago.
  • For the Easter Vacation 2018 school break, we hosted a ‘Football Camp”.
  • December 2017 project focused on Orphans, Widows and Less Fortunate persons. Overall, we provided Food Items, Care Packages consisting of toiletries and a personal Christmas Gift to 94 persons in the six (6) Homes and Centres: (1) Margaret Kistow’s Home for Children (2) Teen Challenge, a Drug rehabilitation Centre for male adults (3) Myrtle’s Safe Haven (4) Omega Home for the Aged (5) Sandra’s Heavenly Haven Home for the Aged (6) Nicare Home for the Aged, Tobago.
  • The July/August project was a 2-week Vacation Camp from 10th to 21st July 2017. 26 Children attended the Camp and provided below is a summary of the achievements:
    • Daily, the Camp started with Prayer and the Children were given a Scripture Verse from the Bible. The Children were taught the Ten Commandments and they sang the National Anthem, and recited the National Pledge, National Watch Words and National Motto.
    • We took the Children on 3 Field Trips – (1) WASA Educational Centre in St. Joseph where they were taught about “Water Conservation” and toured the Facility to understand the various aspects of Water. (2) Airguard Services – At this facility, the children learned about the Services offered and they toured one of the Airplanes and a Helicopter. (3) Bonanza Horse Farm where the children toured the Horse Stables and rode the Horses.
    • On the final day, we presented each Child with a 'Back to School' stationery package consisting of pens, pencils, ruler, eraser, sharpener copybooks, educational book, drawing book, coloured pencils. In addition to the basic stationery items, we gave a geometry set, a Bible and note books and a sketch pad to 7 Children attending Secondary School. We also presented an ‘eco green bag’ to each child which was donated by the Ministry of Social Development and Family Services.
    • Closing Ceremony - We invited Parents and Guardians to the Closing Ceremony on the final day of the Camp. The Children sang the National Anthem and recited the National Pledge, National Watch Words and National Motto. The Children danced to the “Electric Slide” and one Child did a solo Dance to “Still I Rise” by Yolanda Adams, and all the Children sang “Heal the World”. The program ended with the Parents presenting Certificates of Participation to the Children, followed by the Children presenting personalized ‘Thank You Cards’ to the Tzion Gate Volunteers.
  • For the 2017 Easter School Vacation, we sponsored 5 pre-teens and teens from 3 Primary Schools and 2 Secondary Schools to attend a Self-Development Easter Camp hosted by A.P. Toussaint which covered topics such as Anger Management, Talent Development, Dining and Social Etiquette, Public Speaking. The Camp ended with a Graduation Ceremony with parents and guardians in attendance.
  • In February 2017, we donated approximately 50 tins of canned foods each to two of our Associate Members (1) Teen Challenge – a Drug Rehabilitation Centre and (2) Elpis – a Pro-Life Centre.
  • In January 2017, we provided 25 Food Hampers and Clothes for Babies to Adults both male and female to the displaced people of the Leeward side of St. Vincent which was destroyed by a Hurricane in November 2016.
  • For the 2016 Christmas break which was our inaugural Project, we presented “Christmas Bags” filled with Christmas goodies to 320 students of San Juan Boys Primary School and San Juan Girls Primary School.
